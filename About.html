About the Event

History and Significance

The Cultural Heritage Festival began in 2005 as a celebration of Lesotho’s rich traditions and vibrant communities. It showcases music, dance, crafts, and culinary delights from across the country, preserving heritage and passing it on to future generations. Over the years, the festival has grown into one of Lesotho’s most anticipated cultural events, attracting thousands of visitors each year.

For Tourists

Visitors from around the world are invited to experience Lesotho’s unique culture firsthand. Explore traditional performances, taste authentic local cuisine, and engage with artisans in interactive workshops. The festival provides a perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the heart of Lesotho’s traditions while enjoying the scenic beauty of Maseru.

Event Highlights

  • Exhibitions of local art and crafts
